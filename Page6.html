Individual Awards in the World Cup

The Awards







  • The golden boot is given to the top goal scorer in the World Cup tournament
  • The golden glove is given to the most cleansheets and performance by a goal keeper
  • The young player award is given to the best player that is under the age of 21
  • The golden ball is given to the overall best player in the World Cup
